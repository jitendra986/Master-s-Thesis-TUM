The main file to compile is “main_slides.tex”. In there, you also indicate the basic data (presentation purpose, title, conference, authors, etc.).

The contents of the slides are written in the file “slides_contents.tex”.

The bibliography is in “bibfile.bib”.

If you need to include new packages, write them in “preamble.tex”.

Do not modify “setup.tex”
